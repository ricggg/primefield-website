FAQ

Common Questions

What are your delivery areas?

We deliver to all areas within Delta State and neighboring states including Edo, Anambra, Rivers, and Bayelsa. For other locations, please contact us for special arrangements.

How do I place a bulk order?

For bulk orders, you can call us directly, send a WhatsApp message, or fill out the contact form above. We'll respond with pricing and availability within hours.

Do you offer credit facilities?

Yes, we offer flexible credit terms for established wholesale partners. New customers can apply for credit after 3 months of consistent orders with good payment history.

Can I visit your farm?

Absolutely! We welcome farm visits. Please schedule your visit by calling us in advance so we can arrange a proper tour of our facilities.
