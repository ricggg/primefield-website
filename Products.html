FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us directly at +234 91 2808 2080, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the order form on our contact page. We'll confirm your order and arrange delivery.

What is the minimum order quantity?

For eggs, minimum order is 1 crate (30 eggs). For catfish, minimum is 5kg. For day-old chicks, minimum is 50 chicks. Wholesale orders have different minimums - contact us for details.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery within Delta State and neighboring areas. Delivery is free for orders above ₦50,000. For smaller orders, a nominal delivery fee applies based on location.

How fresh are your products?

All our products are farm-fresh. Eggs are collected and sorted daily. Catfish are harvested on order to ensure maximum freshness. We guarantee quality on all deliveries.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cash on delivery (within certain areas), and POS payments. For wholesale partners, we offer flexible credit terms upon approval.
