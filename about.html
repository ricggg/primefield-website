Our Story

A Legacy of Quality & Trust

Founded in 2009, PrimeField Ltd began as a small family-owned poultry farm with a vision to provide the freshest, highest quality eggs to local communities.

Over the years, we've grown from a modest operation with just 500 birds to a modern agricultural enterprise with over 50,000 laying hens and state-of-the-art aquaculture facilities housing thousands of catfish.

Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across Nigeria, from individual households to major hotels, restaurants, and supermarket chains.
