Our Story

A Legacy of Quality Farming

Founded in 2006, PrimeField Limited began as a small family poultry farm with a big dream – to provide the freshest, highest quality eggs and catfish to our community. What started with just 500 birds has grown into one of the region's most trusted agricultural enterprises.

Our journey has been driven by an unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We've invested in modern farming technologies while preserving the traditional values that make farm-fresh products so special.

Today, we serve hundreds of businesses and thousands of families with premium eggs and fresh catfish. Our state-of-the-art facilities, expert team, and dedication to excellence ensure that every product bearing the PrimeField name meets the highest standards.

Chief Emeka Okonkwo

Founder & CEO, PrimeField Limited

Our Mission

To provide premium quality eggs and catfish through sustainable farming practices, ensuring food safety, customer satisfaction, and positive impact on our community while maintaining affordable prices.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural enterprise in Nigeria, recognized for excellence in poultry and fish farming, and to inspire a new generation of sustainable farmers across Africa.

Our Promise

We promise to deliver only the freshest, highest quality products to your table. Every egg and every fish undergoes strict quality control to ensure you receive nothing but the best.
